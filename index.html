Our Story

Founded in 1985, Dakshin Flavors has been serving authentic South Indian cuisine for over 40 years. What started as a small tiffin center has grown into a beloved restaurant, known for its commitment to traditional recipes and fresh ingredients.


We serve authentic South Indian cuisine made with traditional recipes, fresh ingredients, and homemade spices. Our goal is to bring the true taste of South India to every plate. From the aroma of freshly brewed filter coffee to the sizzle of a perfectly crispy dosa — every detail is crafted with love and tradition.
